Title: Bridging Crystal Structure and Material Properties via Bond-Centric Descriptors
Source: arXiv
Date: 2026-03-20T03:16:50.510018

Although chemical bonding is the fundamental mechanistic bridge connecting atomic structure to macroscopic material properties, current data-driven materials science largely treats it as an implicit "black box". Existing machine learning (ML) models rely predominantly on geometric coordinates, forcing them to implicitly relearn complex quantum mechanics from scratch.
