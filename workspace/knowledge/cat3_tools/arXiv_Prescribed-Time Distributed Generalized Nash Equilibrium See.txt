Title: Prescribed-Time Distributed Generalized Nash Equilibrium Seeking
Source: arXiv
Date: 2026-03-18T22:11:13.751283

This paper proposes the first fully distributed algorithm for finding the Generalized Nash Equilibrium (GNE) of a non-cooperative game with shared coupling constraints and general cost coupling at a user-prescribed finite time T.
