[2026-03-17] Diameter Computation on (Random) Geometric Graphs
We present an algorithm that computes the diameter of random geometric graphs (RGGs) with expected average degree $Θ(n^δ)$ for constant $δ\in(0,1)$ in $\tilde{O}(n^{\frac{3}{2}(1+δ)} +n^{2 - \frac{5}{3}δ})$ time, asymptotically almost surely. This brings the running time down to $\tilde{O}(n^{\frac{33}{19}})\approx \tilde{O}(n^{1.737})$ for average degree $Θ(n^{3/19})$.
