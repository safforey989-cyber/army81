Title: Inference in Regression Discontinuity Designs with Clustered Data
Source: arXiv
Date: 2026-03-20T03:17:07.665729

Clustered sampling is prevalent in empirical regression discontinuity (RD) designs, but it has not received much attention in the theoretical literature. In this paper, we introduce a general model-based framework for such settings and derive high-level conditions under which the standard local linear RD estimator is asymptotically normal.
