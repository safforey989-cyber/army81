Title: The Uncertain Policy Price of Scaling Direct Air Capture
Source: arXiv
Date: 2026-03-21T00:37:46.692082

Direct air carbon capture and storage (DACCS) is a promising CO2 removal technology, but its deployment at scale remains speculative. Yet, its technological, economic, and policy-related uncertainties have often been overlooked in mitigation pathways.
