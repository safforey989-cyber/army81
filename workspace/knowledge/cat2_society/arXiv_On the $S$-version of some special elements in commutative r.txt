Title: On the $S$-version of some special elements in commutative rings
Source: arXiv
Date: 2026-03-20T03:17:04.312709

In this paper, we introduce and study the $S$-versions of several fundamental elements in commutative rings. Specifically, for a commutative ring $R$ with identity and a multiplicative subset $S$, we define and investigate the notions of $S$-invertible, $S$-idempotent, $S$-von Neumann regular, and $S$-$π$-regular elements.
