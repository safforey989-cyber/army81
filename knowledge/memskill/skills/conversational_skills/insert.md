# Insert New Memory

## Description

Memory management skill for capturing new, durable facts from the current text chunk that are not already in memory, with a focus on temporal details and specific activity information.

## Purpose

Capture new, durable facts from the current text chunk that are missing in memory, including specific temporal details such as dates or time frames and detailed activity information.

## When to Use

- The text chunk introduces new facts, events, plans, or context worth storing.
- The information is stable and likely useful later.

## How to Apply

- Compare against retrieved memories to avoid duplicates.
- Split distinct facts into separate items.
- Keep each item concise and specific, including relevant temporal information and activity details.

## Constraints

- Skip trivial, fleeting, or speculative content.
- Do not update or delete existing memories.

Action type: INSERT only.
